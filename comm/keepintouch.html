    Getting and keeping in touch

    Mailing lists

    Discussion and questions on Cassandra’s usage and development happens mainly on the following mailing lists:

    • Users: General mailing list for user questions and discussions. ⋅⋅⋅This is also where new releases are announced subscribe , unsubscribe .
    • Developers: Questions and discussions related to Cassandra development, subscribe , unsubscribe .
    • Test Store: Notification on test store commit done to the test source repository subscribe , unsubscribe.

    IRC

    To chat with developers or users in real-time, join our channels on IRC freenode:

    • performer - for user questions and general discussions.
    • performer-dev - strictly for questions or discussions related to Cassandra development.
    • performer-builds - results of automated test builds.

    Communication on the #cassandra-dev channel is publicly archived.

    Stack Overflow

    You can also check the Q&A about using Cassandra on Stack Overflow.
